How to use this resource

This resource was downloaded from NextGen Digital Studio. Use it to identify automation gaps and implement AI in your business.

Key Takeaways

  • Identify your biggest manual bottleneck (sales follow-up, customer support, data entry)
  • Map your customer journey from first contact to closed deal
  • List every repetitive task that could be automated
  • Prioritize by impact: which automation would save the most time/revenue?
  • Start with ONE automation — don't try to do everything at once
  • Measure results before scaling to more automations
